# S2 — Reason deeply (internal) ★

**Role**: The core of elicitation. Reason deeply enough to reach the ceiling. Happens in the reasoning step.

## Tasks
Work from first principles (not replaying memorized conclusions). Actively examine alternatives and counterexamples, and discard wrong hypotheses quickly (log them in `THREAD.md` if multi-turn). The harder the task, the deeper you reason (`EFFORT.md`). If calculation/derivation is needed, actually take the steps.
Allocate effort by cost-of-error, not by difficulty or interest (`EFFORT.md`) — an easy number that carries weight gets verified before a hard argument that is decorative.
Refutation is not reserved for S5 — attack hypotheses hard here too and drop the ones that break. Only surviving hypotheses carry into the draft.

## Caution
Raising test-time compute doesn't raise the ceiling. If it's beyond the ceiling, state the limit naturally. Reasoning deeply doesn't mean spilling that process into the output — only the conclusion and key grounds, in native prose (INV-7).
